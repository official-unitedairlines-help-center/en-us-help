Live Chat

Use the live chat feature on the United Airlines website via the "Help" or "Contact Us" sections for real-time assistance.

Mobile App

The United Airlines mobile app allows direct access to phone and chat support while on the go.

Email Support

For non-urgent issues, email United Airlines. Response times may vary from 24 to 48 hours.
